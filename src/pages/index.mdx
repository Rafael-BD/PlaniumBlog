# Privacy Policy for Planium App

**Effective Date:** January 17, 2024

## 1. Information Collected

### 1.1 User Information
When using the Planium app, we collect and store personal information, including but not limited to, username, email address, password, and user preferences.

### 1.2 Location Data
To provide features such as weather forecasts and traffic information, the app may collect location data from the device.

### 1.3 Usage Data
We record information about the app's usage, including interactions with tasks, user preferences, and data related to the use of artificial intelligence.

## 2. Purposes of Collection

### 2.1 App Operation
We use the collected information to operate, maintain, and improve the functionality of the app, including customizing recommendations and alerts.

### 2.2 Communications
We may use the provided email address to send notifications, app-related information, updates, and special offers.

## 3. Data Sharing

### 3.1 Third Parties
We do not share personal information with third parties, except when necessary to provide specific services, such as integration with weather and traffic services.

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures
We implement security measures to protect user information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or unauthorized destruction.

## 5. User Rights

### 5.1 Access and Modification
Users have the right to access and modify their personal information through the app's settings.

### 5.2 Account Deletion
Users can request the deletion of their accounts, resulting in the automatic deletion of their data.

## 6. Privacy Policy Updates

### 6.1 Change Notifications
Users will be notified of changes to this Privacy Policy through in-app notifications or email.

## 7. Contact

### 7.1 User Support
To contact user support or ask questions related to this Privacy Policy, use the following email address: planium.support@proton.me.
